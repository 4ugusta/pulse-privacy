Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

Pulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Phone number (for authentication)
  • Name (optional display name)
  • Email address (optional)

Location Data

With your permission, we collect location data to enable location sharing with your connected partner. You can disable location sharing at any time in the app settings. Location data is only shared with users you have explicitly connected with.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you use the app, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App interactions and features used
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's features
  • Enable communication between connected users
  • Send push notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Display relevant advertisements (for free users)

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Connected Users: Your status, location (if enabled), and notifications are shared only with users you have accepted connections with.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services including Firebase (authentication, push notifications, analytics), Stripe (payment processing), and Google AdMob (advertising).

Advertising

Free users may see advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and use advertising IDs to show relevant ads. Premium subscribers do not see advertisements. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Pulse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of location sharing
  • Disable push notifications

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
