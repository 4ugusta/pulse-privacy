Pulse - Child Safety Standards

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Our Commitment

Pulse is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or endangers children.

Age Requirements

Pulse is designed for adults aged 18 and older. Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account and use our services. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on Pulse:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Content that sexualizes minors
  • Content that promotes or glorifies child exploitation
  • Grooming behavior or attempts to contact minors inappropriately
  • Any content that endangers children

Detection and Prevention

We employ the following measures to prevent child exploitation:

  • Age verification during account registration
  • User reporting mechanisms for inappropriate content
  • Review of reported content by our team
  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Permanent account termination for violators

Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report child safety concerns through:

  • In-app reporting feature
  • Email: b.augusta1103@gmail.com

All reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken.

Cooperation with Authorities

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and report any discovered CSAM to:

  • The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Relevant local and national authorities

Enforcement

Violations of our child safety standards result in:

  • Immediate content removal
  • Permanent account termination
  • Reporting to law enforcement authorities
  • Cooperation with any resulting investigations
